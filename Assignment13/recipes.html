Classic Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning

Instructions

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  3. In a skillet, cook the ground beef until browned. Add marinara sauce and stir.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, 1 cup mozzarella, Parmesan, salt, and Italian seasoning.
  5. Layer the lasagna in a baking dish: noodles, meat sauce, cheese mixture. Repeat layers.
  6. Top with the remaining mozzarella cheese.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for 10 more minutes until bubbly.
  8. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
