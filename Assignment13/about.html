Hello, I'm Hannah!

I'm your average at-home chef and baker, learning everything I know from my family, friends, and Google. Cooking and baking bring me so much joy, and I love sharing my favorite recipes with others.

As an out of state student, I always find ways to make me feel more connected to my family back in Washington. Growing up I spent every holiday baking with my grandparents and extended family. I was also in charge of dinners for my family at least once a week. I use the kitchen as a way to help upkeep traditions when I'm not home!

Trust me when I say I'm not perfect. There have been many kitchen mishaps. Such as almsot lighting the kitchen on fire while frying eggs, or not the one, but two times I have forgotten at least half of the required measurement for flower in my chocolate chip cookies.
