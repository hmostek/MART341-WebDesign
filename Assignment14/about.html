Hello, I'm Hannah!

I'm your average at-home chef and baker, learning everything I know from my family, friends, and Google. Cooking and baking bring me so much joy, and I love sharing my favorite recipes with others.

As an out-of-state student, I always find ways to feel more connected to my family back in Washington. Growing up, I spent every holiday baking with my grandparents and extended family. I was also in charge of dinners for my family at least once a week. The kitchen helps me uphold traditions even when I'm not home!

Trust me when I say I'm not perfect. There have been many kitchen mishaps, like almost lighting the kitchen on fire while frying eggs or the two times I forgot half the flour in my chocolate chip cookies.
